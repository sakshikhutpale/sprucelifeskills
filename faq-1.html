Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section of Spruce Lifeskills! We understand you may have questions about our specialized programs in Medical Coding and Clinical Research. This page is designed to provide you with clear and concise answers to the most common queries about our courses, certifications, career opportunities, and how we help you excel in these dynamic fields. Whether you're just starting your journey or looking to enhance your skills, our goal is to ensure you have all the information you need to make confident decisions. If you don’t find what you’re looking for here, feel free to reach out to our team – we’re here to help!

How can I be so sure that Spruce's courses will help me to advance my career?
Spruce is Central India’s exclusive AAPC-authorized training institute, ensuring our courses meet industry standards and are recognized by employers. Our comprehensive curriculum, experienced instructors, and focus on practical skills provide you with the knowledge and credentials needed to excel in the healthcare industry. Additionally, many of our graduates have successfully advanced their careers, securing rewarding positions in various healthcare.
Can I take Spruce's courses online, or are they only available in person?
We offer both online and in-person course formats to accommodate your needs. Whether you prefer the flexibility of online learning or the interactive experience of in-person classes, Spruce provides high-quality education in the format that works best for you.
How long does it take to complete a course at Spruce?
The duration of our courses varies depending on the specific program and your chosen pace of study. Generally, our courses can be completed within a few months to a year. Detailed information about the length of each course is available on our course pages or by contacting our admissions team.
Will I receive support and guidance during and after the course?
Absolutely. At Spruce, we are committed to your success. Our instructors and support staff are available to provide assistance and guidance throughout your learning journey. Additionally, we offer career services and alumni support to help you navigate job opportunities and career advancement after completing your course.
What are the prerequisites and eligibility criteria for enrolling in a course?
Prerequisites and eligibility criteria vary by course. Generally, you need a basic educational background and an interest in healthcare. Some courses may require specific prior knowledge or experience. Detailed prerequisites for each course are listed on our course pages, or you can contact our admissions office for personalized guidance.
